Shredded Chicken Tacos

Description

These tacos are simple to make and even simpler to enjoy.

With only 3 key ingredients, you can create these delicious tacos with ease

Ingredients

  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 3 tablespoons taco seasoning
  • 1 pound skinless, boneless chicken breasts

Steps

  1. Combine chicken broth with taco seasoning mix in a bowl.
  2. Place chicken in a slow cooker. Pour chicken broth mixture over chicken.
  3. Cook on Low for 6 to 8 hours. Shred chicken.
  4. Spoon into warmed corn tortillas. Garnish with shredded lettuce, chopped tomatoes, and thinly sliced red onions, if desired.
